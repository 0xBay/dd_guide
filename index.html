0. Brokerage 🏦 It’s important to have a brokerage that will fill you properly, doesn’t crash on high volume, and doesn’t sell your orders

Basically this means GET OFF ROBINHOOD, this should be the first thing you do on your journey to improve your trading. The only thing RobinHood is useful for imo is as a checking account or fun/yolo account, other than that some good brokerages are: ThinkOrSwim, TradeStation, TastyWorks, and Webull — check out these if you’re on RobinHood and make the switch ASAP if you plan on trading options (I use TOS)

Another note on brokerages 👉🏼 If you are trading with under $25K, make sure you open a cash account and NOT a margin account, this is crucial

3. Watch lists 👀

There are two steps to creating a good watchlist system IMO (#2 is more important than #1 focus on that)

The platform you have your watchlists on

  • I would say just use your broker, I use Webull because I like how it looks and how I can swipe through the best, this is gonna mainly be personal preference.

How you organize your watch lists

  • It's important to have organized watchlists, here is my system:

I separate my watch lists by market cap, categories, and a focus list that changes weekly of the top 4-8 stocks I'm focusing on that week. Here are all the different watch lists I use (you dont have to name them exactly how I do)👇

  1. FOCUS — Focus list consists of the 4-8 stocks I am focusing on this week, drawn from one of my other watchlists
  1. ETF's — This is a small list that contains the main ETF's traders follow (SPY, QQQ, SQQQ VXX, SLV, GLD) you could also throw ES in here as well
  1. MEGA CAP — companies with market caps over $100B (i.e. AAPL,BABA,MSFT,ZM)
  1. LARGE CAP — companies with martket caps between $10B-$99B (i.e. LMND, CHWY,DKNG,EBAY,LULU,ROKU,SNAP)
  1. MID CAP — companies with market caps between $1B-$9B (i.e. SPCE,JMIA,OSTK,FVRR,TTCF)
  1. SMOL CAP — companies with market caps under $1B (i.e. MARA,RIOT,NLS,LOVE,VUZI,XSPA,MVIS)
  1. ELECTRIC CITY — electric vehicle and solar companies (i.e. NIO,QS,FCEL,PLUG,BLNK,FSR,GOEV)
  1. SPAC's — the title says it all, this is my SPAC watchlist 🌶️
